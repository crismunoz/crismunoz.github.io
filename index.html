About

Cristian E. Muñoz Villalobos was born in Lima, Peru in 1988. In 2010 he graduated in Mechatronic Engineering at the National University of Engineering. He received his M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering in 2014 at Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro. Nowadays, he is a PhD student from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro, in Computational intelligence specialization.
